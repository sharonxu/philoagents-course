What is PhiloAgents?

PhiloAgents is an AI-powered game simulation where you explore a charming pixel-art world and have meaningful conversations with history's most influential thinkers.

Each philosopher is powered by advanced AI agents using LangGraph and RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation). They don't just recite facts‚Äîthey engage you in authentic dialogue based on their actual philosophical perspectives.
